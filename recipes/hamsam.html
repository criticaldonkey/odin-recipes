A Simple Ham Sandwich

A delicious and nutritious ham sandwhich.

This is pretty basic, but healthfully filling if you include fruit (tomato) and greens. You'll need:

  • 2 oz thin-sliced ham
  • One tablespoon mayonnaise
  • One tablespoon dijon mustard
  • One small tomato
  • Favorite leafy greens
  • Whole grain bread

Steps:

  1. Slice the tomato in thin rounds. You need only enough to cover the sandwich; keep the remainder for tomorrow.
  2. Thinly spread the mayo and mustard across both slices of bread.
  3. Stack the ingredients, with bread slices on the outside and mayo-mustard facing in.
