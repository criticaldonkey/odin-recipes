A Great Salad

A delicious and nutritious avocado salad.

Avocado is widely regarded as a "power food" due to the quality of vitamins and healthy fats. To make this delicous dish, you'll need:

  • Two fairly firm avocados
  • One tablespoon of lime juice
  • One small tomato
  • Roasted tortilla strips (store bought or home made)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps:

  1. Carefully open the avocado and remove the seed. Slice the meat into rows then columns to form nice cubes. Use a spoon to scoop these into serving bowl.
  2. Dice the tomato and add to the serving bowl.
  3. Spritz with lime juice.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Garnish with tortilla strips.
